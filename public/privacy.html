Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Richard Thorneycroft, trading as Teknon ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use the Teknon application and website (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at thorneycroft.r@gmail.com.

Who we are

The data controller for Teknon is Richard Thorneycroft, operating as a sole trader in England and Wales. You can contact us at thorneycroft.r@gmail.com.

What data we collect

We collect the following personal data when you use Teknon:

  • Email address — collected when you sign up for a subscription, used to manage your account and verify your subscription status
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Artwork images — images you upload for analysis are processed by the Anthropic API to generate feedback and are not stored on our servers beyond the duration of your session
  • Session data — your analyses, feedback and session history are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly use features that transmit it.
  • Usage data — we may collect anonymised data about how the Service is used, including page views and feature usage, via Vercel Analytics

How we use your data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Teknon Service
  • To manage your subscription and process payments via Stripe
  • To verify your subscription status when you use the Service
  • To communicate with you about your subscription, including trial expiry and billing
  • To improve the Service based on anonymised usage patterns

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — processing your email and payment data is necessary to provide the subscription service you have signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — we have a legitimate interest in understanding how our Service is used in order to improve it
  • Legal obligation — we may process data where required to comply with applicable law

Your artwork and feedback

We take the privacy of your creative work seriously. Images you upload to Teknon are transmitted to the Anthropic API solely for the purpose of generating feedback. They are not stored on our servers, not shared with third parties for any other purpose, not used to train AI models, and not visible to anyone other than you.

This is your private studio. The door closes behind you.

Third parties

We use the following third-party services to operate Teknon:

  • Anthropic — processes artwork images and text to generate mentor feedback. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy
  • Stripe — processes subscription payments. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy
  • Vercel — hosts the Teknon application. Vercel's privacy policy is available at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

Data retention

We retain your email address and subscription records for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for legal and accounting purposes. Session data stored in your browser's localStorage remains on your device and can be cleared by you at any time by clearing your browser data.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at thorneycroft.r@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Teknon does not use cookies. We use browser localStorage to store your session data and subscription status locally on your device. This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to us.

Children

Teknon is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify subscribers of any significant changes by email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.
